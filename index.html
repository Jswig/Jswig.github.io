Bio

I am a third-year undergraduate at UC Santa Cruz, working towards a double major in computer science and mathematics. My main interests reside in machine learning, Bayesian statistics and numerical optimization. Outside of class, I am the vice-president of Data Science Slugs, a campus organization seeking to prepare students for a career in data science, where I lead coding workshops and work on Kaggle competitions.
